The Best Movies According to Maalik

My top movies of all time:

In the Heights

Top-tier musical chronicling the convergence of many different lives in New York City.

Harry Potter movie series

The first novel debuted while I was in middle school. I was on the waiting list to read each new release, aha. The movies tied together my imaginative eye with the author's vision.

The Pursuit of Happiness

This movie has inspired me to remain persistent as I strive for my professional ambitions. It's about the journey a father takes to secure comfortability for his family.
